favorite place you can see in Egypt

pyramids

Pyramids are ancient monumental structures, most famously in Egypt and Sudan (Nubian), built primarily as, tombs for pharaohs and royalty during the Old and Middle Kingdoms.

khan el khalili

Khan el-Khalili is a famous 14th-century bazaar and souq in the heart of Islamic Cairo, Egypt, known for its , labyrinthine alleys, historic Mamluk-style architecture, and bustling, lively atmosphere. It is a premier shopping destination for spices, jewelry, perfumes, handicrafts, and souvenirs, and it is best visited the evening when shops are open and cafes are buzzing.
